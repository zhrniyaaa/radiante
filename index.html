About Radiante

More than a salon. A space where beauty meets intention.

We believe beauty is personal.

Founded in 2014 in Almaty, Radiante was born from a single conviction — that a great haircut can change how you move through the world. We set out to create a salon that felt less like a transaction and more like a ritual.

Over ten years, we've grown to four locations across the city, each one designed as a sanctuary. Warm lighting, curated music, and a team that genuinely listens before they ever pick up the scissors.

Our stylists train continuously — internationally and locally — so that whatever's happening in fashion or hair culture, we're ready to bring it to you with precision and care.
